The Story
Born From 4 Years of Gifting

It started in a garage in West Palm Beach. Dave Wallis spent four years handcrafting coastal centerpieces for friends, family, and high-end clients. Glass dishes filled with sand, hand-painted sea turtle hatchlings, starfish, shells — little beaches you could hold in your hands.

Every single one got the same reaction: "Where can I buy this?"

Hatchling Beach is the answer. Premium DIY coastal centerpiece kits that bring the Florida coast into any room. Not kitschy beach decor — elevated, warm, personal. Every kit is a story waiting to be arranged.
