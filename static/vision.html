The Opportunity

A Market Gap Hiding in Plain Sight

Beach decor is a perennial top-5 home decor theme. Sea turtles are universally loved, conservation-aligned, and Instagram-ready. DIY personalization is booming.

Yet no one makes a premium DIY turtle hatchling beach kit. Not on Amazon. Not on Etsy. Not in stores. The niche is completely open.

Founder Dave Wallis has been handcrafting these as gifts for South Florida's most exclusive clients for five years — from Greg Norman's estate to courtside at Miami Heat games. Every single person who received one loved it.
